We don't collect, store, or track any of your personal data. Your tasks stay on your device, period.

What We Don't Collect

Unlike many applications, Cascade is designed with privacy at its core. We don't collect:

  • Your tasks or personal information
  • Your email address or contact details
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device information or browser data
  • Location data or personal preferences
  • Cookies for tracking purposes

How Your Data Works

Local Storage Only: All your tasks are stored directly in your browser's local storage. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • We can't see what tasks you create
  • No servers store your personal information
  • You have complete control over your data

What We Do Collect

For basic functionality and security, our hosting service may collect:

  • Basic server logs: IP addresses and request times (automatically deleted after 30 days)
  • Technical information: Only what's needed to serve the website (no personal data)

This is standard for any website and is handled by our hosting provider, not by us directly.

Data Export & Import

You have full control over your data:

  • Export: Download your tasks as a JSON file anytime
  • Import: Restore or transfer your tasks to another device
  • Delete: Clear all data through your browser settings

Third-Party Services

Cascade uses minimal third-party services:

  • Google Fonts: For typography (may set cookies per Google's policy)
  • CloudFront CDN: For fast, reliable delivery (AWS standard logging)

These services have their own privacy policies, but they don't have access to your task data.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there's not much to manage! But you can:

  • Use Cascade without creating an account
  • Export your data at any time
  • Delete your data by clearing browser storage
  • Contact us with any privacy questions

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change how we handle privacy (spoiler: we won't), we'll update this page and change the effective date above.

Questions?

If you have any questions about privacy or how your data is handled, feel free to reach out through our support channels.
