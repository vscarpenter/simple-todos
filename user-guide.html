🚀 Quick Start

Privacy First: Cascade runs entirely in your browser. Your data stays on your device - nothing is sent to any server.

🎯 Getting Started

📝 Adding Tasks

  1. Type your task in the input field at the top
  2. Click "Add Task" or press Enter
  3. Tasks appear in the "To Do" column

⚡ Managing Tasks

  • Drag & drop between columns
  • Edit by clicking the Edit button
  • Delete unwanted tasks
  • Move with arrow buttons
